Janky Earth Defense

Draw shields (white lines) by clicking and dragging to intercept the meteors (red lines) and protect the Earth

Every line is limited to a length of 100px and you must wait for your power to regenerate to draw longer lines. Shorter Lines earn more points!

Every 10,000 points will reward you with another health point

Try to surive for as long as possible!

Advanced tip: you can draw shields to intesect the meteors to destroy them instantly!
